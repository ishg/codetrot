About Me

What's up, Dear Reader. I'm Ishmeet Grewal, an undergraduate student at The Ohio State University. With a major in Computer Science and Engineering, I harbor a strong passion for programming and have recently become interested in web design. By creating this website, I aim to develop web design skills as well as productively pass my summer.

I am also a keen traveller, and this website serves the additional purpose of sharing my travels around the world with my audience. I started this project after a recent trip to Appalachia and hope to continue making it better once my upcoming trip to Italy is done. So, stay tuned for more goodies!
